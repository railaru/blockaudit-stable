Build bullet proof databases

Blockaudit is a decentralized platform that allows you to create databases. It does all the dirty work for you by automatically setting up blockchain based backend service allowing you to focus on what matters.

The backend of the DAPP will be built on the Cardano blockchain, the first blockchain platform to evolve out of a scientific philosophy and a research-first driven approach. It was developed by a large global collective of expert engineers and researchers.

This will enable developers to create records of: bank statements, medical history, investment performance, legislation, goverment budgets and even historical events and dates audited and verified on a public ledger. Once the records are created, they can't be edited or manipulated. All done without a third party that has to be trusted.

The project is open source based, ready for contributions from great minds across the globe.

Blockchain enables trustless exchange of ideas, capital, records information and more.

Redefine record keeping

A digital blockchain property register that identified land using GPS coordinates would allow property ownership to be verified and transferred at low cost. Some third world countries like Rwanda have already woken up to this, and as part of its digital transformation plan is looking to port its ledger onto the blockchain. There are similar noises in both Kenya and Ghana, as government officials begin to see that the technology might let them leapfrog the legacy systems that are currently used in the western world.

Using the blockaudit decentralized application, you can easily build landmark records and publish them on your blockchain off choice.

Blockchain technology can be used to securely store health records and maintain a single version of the truth. The different organisations such as doctors, hospitals, laboratories, pharmacists and health insurers can request permission to access a patient’s record to serve their purpose and record transactions on the distributed ledger.

Blockaudit provides a simple solution for interacting with a such ledger. The platform is built to securely store and share electronic health records. By digitizing health records and empowering users we can leverage countless industry synergies.
